Open source map of Cubbon Park

OpenStreetMap

or OSM is an online collaborative project to create a free and editable map of the whole world.

Why?

This poster was created to showcase the public benefit of contributing to open source projects. The software and data used to create this poster has been possible due to the millions of volunteer contributions from all parts of the world who are part of the open source community.

And the resulting work is available for you to use, improve, translate and distribute at no cost.

How?

  • Map data: © OpenStreetMap contributors , download India data from Geofabrik , available under ODbL license
  • Tools: TileMill
  • Typeface: Source Sans Pro from Adobe
  • Design: 4thMain

What?

  • Use this map to understand your city
  • Navigate and explore the neighbourhood
  • Improve this map and show the world what’s special here
  • Download the map data and create your own maps
  • Make copies of this map, redistribute it, or sell it
